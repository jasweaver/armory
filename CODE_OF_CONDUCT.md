# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable, specific feedback
- Focus on project goals and user impact

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discrimination or hateful conduct
- Sharing private information without consent
- Disruptive or bad-faith collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct and may limit participation for repeated violations.
